Basics

When the heated debate over whether to buy the latest Playstation or Xbox arrives, the minority decide instead to purchase a PC, but what not many of these people know is that you can construct your own PC with minimal work, rather then to buy a pre-built one! This guide will help you to understand the ins and outs of PC building and the way a system is put together to create a computer that can be hundreds of dollars cheaper than store-assembled ones.

So let's get started!

Cybersafety

Finally, after turning on your newly built PC and being proud that it hasn't blown up yet, here are some important tips to consider.

Make sure to:

Install a free antivirus

Avira

Many free antiviruses are available for all kinds of purposes, and this is the one I recommend. Avira is known for a high detection rate, high performance and high reliability.




Get a password manager

LastPass

Using a password manager is part of keeping your identity safe online. It makes it harder to gain access to all your accounts, especially if you used to use just one password for all of them!




Keep your PC junk-free

CCleaner

Using software such as CCleaner keeps your PC up and running while making sure to get rid of all that unnecessary junk gathering up on your hard drive.
